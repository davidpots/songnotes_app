Chorus Walk-Down & Walk-Up
Here is an optional, subtle way to approximate the "oh, oh, oh..." part on an acoustic guitar. You'll want to bar your finger across the 2nd fret on the second "oh...", and then move your ring finger over the 4th fret on the third "oh". Listen along for reference, and check out my video lesson to see how this is done.
